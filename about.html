The Founder

Meet Lauren Bennett

Lauren Bennett started SYNQ OS after spending years watching talented service business owners struggle — not because they lacked skill, but because they lacked systems. The tools existed, but nobody was putting them together in a way that actually worked.

She built SYNQ OS to change that. Not just another agency — a true operating system for your business. One team, one strategy, one set of systems engineered to work together and drive real, measurable growth.

Today, SYNQ OS serves service businesses across the country — from solo operators just getting started to established companies ready to scale beyond their current ceiling.
