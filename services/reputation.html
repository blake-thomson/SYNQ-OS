FAQ

Common Questions About Reputation Management

Can you remove negative reviews?
We can't remove legitimate reviews — and neither can anyone else ethically. What we can do is respond professionally to negative reviews (which shows prospects you care), resolve issues privately, and flood your profile with new positive reviews so the negatives are drowned out.
Won't asking for reviews annoy my customers?
Not when done right. Our requests are timed for peak satisfaction (right after a great experience), personalized, and sent through the channel they prefer. Most customers are happy to help — they just need to be asked. Our open rates on review requests consistently exceed 60%.
Which platforms do you focus on?
Google is always the priority because it directly impacts local search rankings. Beyond that, we monitor and manage Facebook, Yelp, and any industry-specific platforms relevant to your business. The strategy is customized based on where your customers actually look.
How quickly will I see more reviews coming in?
Most clients see new reviews within the first week of launch. The automated system runs after every customer interaction, so review velocity scales with your business volume. Clients typically see a 3-5x increase in monthly reviews within the first 30 days.
Is this compliant with Google's review policies?
Absolutely. We follow all platform guidelines — no incentivized reviews, no fake reviews, no review gating that violates Google's terms. We simply make it easy for real customers to share their real experience, which is exactly what every platform encourages.
