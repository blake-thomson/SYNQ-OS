FAQ

Common Questions
About Web Design

How long does a website build take?
Most projects take 3-6 weeks from kickoff to launch. Simple landing pages can be done in under two weeks. Full multi-page sites with custom funnels typically land closer to 4-6 weeks depending on scope and revision cycles.
Do you use templates or build custom?
Everything is custom. We design from scratch based on your brand, your market, and your conversion goals. No WordPress themes, no Wix templates. That's how we get the results we do.
Will I be able to edit the site myself after launch?
Yes. We build on platforms that allow you to make simple content updates. For structural changes or new pages, most clients prefer to have us handle it as part of an ongoing retainer — but you always have access.
What's the difference between a website and a funnel?
A website is your full online presence — multiple pages covering your brand, services, and credibility. A funnel is a focused, linear path designed to drive one specific action (like booking a call). Most clients need both, and we build them to work together.
Do you handle the copywriting?
Yes. Every project includes conversion-focused copywriting. We write the headlines, body copy, CTAs, and meta descriptions — all based on your voice, your audience, and what actually converts in your industry.
