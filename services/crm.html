Common Questions
About CRM Buildouts

How long does a full CRM buildout take?
Most buildouts are completed in 2-4 weeks depending on complexity. Simple single-pipeline setups can launch in under two weeks. Multi-location or enterprise builds with complex automation logic typically take closer to four.
Do I need to use GoHighLevel specifically?
We specialize in GoHighLevel because it gives service businesses the best combination of CRM, automation, and communication tools in one platform. If you're on a different platform, we can discuss migration or alternative solutions during our strategy call.
What if I already have a CRM that's half set up?
We'll audit what you have, keep what works, and rebuild what doesn't. Many of our clients come to us with partially configured systems that were never finished properly. We clean them up and make them actually work.
Will my team actually use it?
That's exactly why we include training and SOPs. We build systems that match how your team already works — not the other way around. The easier it is, the more they'll use it. We've never had a client whose team didn't adopt the system.
What happens after launch — do you offer ongoing support?
Yes. We offer ongoing optimization and support packages. The first 60 days post-launch include active monitoring and tuning at no extra charge. After that, most clients keep us on retainer for continued optimization as their business scales.
