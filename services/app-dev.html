How It Works

From Idea to Production in 4 Phases

01

Discovery & Requirements

We sit down with your team and map out exactly what the application needs to do. Workflows, user roles, data models, and integrations — everything is documented in a clear specification before development begins. No ambiguity, no scope creep.

02

Architecture & Prototyping

We design the system architecture, create interactive prototypes, and validate the user experience with your team. You'll see and click through the application before a single line of production code is written.

03

Development & Testing

Agile development in 2-week sprints with regular demos. You see progress constantly and provide feedback along the way. Every feature is tested thoroughly — unit tests, integration tests, and user acceptance testing before launch.

04

Launch, Monitor & Iterate

We deploy to production, monitor performance, and provide immediate support. Post-launch, we collect feedback and iterate. Most applications go through 2-3 improvement cycles in the first 90 days as real usage reveals new opportunities.

FAQ

Common Questions
About App Development

How much does custom software cost?
It depends entirely on complexity. Simple internal tools might start around $10K-$25K. Full SaaS products with user authentication, dashboards, and integrations typically range from $25K-$100K+. We scope every project precisely before development begins so there are no surprises.
How long does development take?
Simple tools can launch in 4-6 weeks. More complex applications typically take 2-4 months. We work in sprints with regular deliverables, so you're not waiting months to see anything — you'll see working software every two weeks.
Do I own the code?
Yes, 100%. Everything we build is yours — source code, documentation, infrastructure access. No lock-in, no proprietary frameworks that force you to stay with us. You own the entire product.
Can you integrate with our existing tools?
Absolutely. API integrations are one of our core strengths. We've connected applications to CRMs, payment processors, accounting systems, marketing platforms, ERPs, and custom databases. If it has an API, we can connect to it.
What happens after the app is built?
We offer ongoing maintenance and support packages. Software needs updates, bug fixes, and new features as your business grows. Most clients keep us on retainer for continuous development — treating the app as a living product, not a one-time project.
