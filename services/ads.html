FAQ

Common Questions About Paid Ads

How much should I spend on ads?
It depends on your market, competition, and goals. Most of our clients start with $2,000-$5,000/month in ad spend and scale from there as we prove ROI. We'll recommend a budget during your strategy call based on your specific situation.
Should I do Meta Ads or Google Ads?
It depends on your business. Google Ads captures people actively searching for your service (high intent). Meta Ads reaches people who fit your ideal customer profile but aren't actively searching (demand generation). Most service businesses benefit from both.
How fast will I see results?
You'll typically see leads within the first 1-2 weeks of launch. The first 30 days are about testing and finding winning combinations. By month 2-3, campaigns are optimized and performance is scaling. Unlike SEO, ads deliver immediate traffic.
What makes you different from other ad agencies?
Transparency and integration. We don't just manage ads — we build the entire system: ads, landing pages, CRM integration, and follow-up automation. You see every dollar spent and every lead generated. No hidden fees, no vague reporting.
Do you handle the creative or do I need to provide it?
We handle everything — ad copy, image selection, video concepts, and landing page design. If you have existing brand assets, we'll work with them. If not, we create everything from scratch as part of the service.
