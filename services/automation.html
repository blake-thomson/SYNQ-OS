FAQ

Common Questions
About Automation

Won't automated messages feel impersonal?
Not the way we build them. Every message is written in your brand's voice with personalization fields like first name, service interest, and specific actions they took. Our clients regularly get replies like "thanks for the quick response" — people can't tell it's automated.
How many emails/texts will my leads receive?
It depends on the workflow, but we're strategic — not spammy. A typical nurture sequence might be 5-8 emails over 2-3 weeks, with SMS used for high-intent moments like appointment confirmations. We always include opt-out mechanisms and respect compliance requirements.
Do I need a CRM already set up?
Not necessarily. If you don't have one, we can build your CRM and automations together as a combined package. If you already have GoHighLevel or a similar platform, we'll build directly inside your existing system.
Is SMS marketing legal? What about compliance?
Yes, when done properly. We follow all TCPA and CAN-SPAM regulations. Every workflow includes proper consent mechanisms, opt-out handling, and compliance checkpoints. We handle the technical side so you stay compliant.
How quickly will I see results?
Most clients see measurable improvement within the first 2 weeks of launch. Speed-to-lead automations show immediate impact. Nurture sequences build momentum over 30-60 days as leads move through the funnel and start converting.
