FAQ

Common Questions
About SEO

How long does SEO take to work?
You'll typically see early movement within 30-60 days, with meaningful rankings and traffic increases by 90 days. SEO is a compounding asset — the longer you invest, the bigger the returns. Most clients see their strongest results at the 6-12 month mark.
Can you guarantee page one rankings?
No ethical SEO provider can guarantee specific rankings — Google's algorithm changes constantly. What we can guarantee is a proven process, transparent reporting, and a track record of getting our clients to page one for their target keywords consistently.
I tried SEO before and it didn't work. Why would this be different?
Most SEO failures come from agencies that focus on vanity metrics instead of revenue. We tie everything to leads and conversions. You'll see exactly which keywords are driving calls and form fills — not just impressions and clicks that don't pay the bills.
Do I need SEO if I'm already running ads?
Absolutely. Ads give you immediate traffic but the moment you stop paying, it stops. SEO builds a permanent asset that generates leads for free, month after month. The best strategy is both working together — ads for now, SEO for long-term compounding growth.
What's included in the monthly reporting?
Keyword ranking changes, organic traffic growth, lead attribution, backlink progress, content performance, and competitive benchmarks. We also include a plain-English summary of what we did, what moved, and what we're focusing on next month.
